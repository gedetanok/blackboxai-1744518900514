How to Play

Game Controls

  • Accelerate
  • Brake
  • Steer Left
  • Steer Right
  • Turbo Boost (Space)

Game Mechanics

  • Slipstream: Drive behind opponents to gain speed boost
  • Corners: Inside lane is faster than outside
  • Turbo: Limited boost per race, use strategically
  • Victory: Finish first after two laps to advance

Race Tracks

Countryside Sprint

A scenic route through rolling hills and challenging curves.

City Lights

Navigate through the bustling streets of a neon-lit metropolis.
